How AI Detects Crop Diseases

AI leverages image recognition and big data to identify diseases from photos of plant leaves. Farmers can upload images via mobile apps to receive instant diagnoses and treatment suggestions.

Benefits

  • Early detection of diseases and pests
  • Prevents crop loss and increases productivity
  • Provides organic and chemical treatment options
  • Alerts for regional disease outbreaks
