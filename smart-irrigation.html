What is Smart Irrigation?

Smart irrigation uses AI, IoT sensors, and weather data to automate and optimize watering schedules. This reduces water waste, saves money, and helps crops thrive even in changing climates.

Features

  • Automated watering based on soil moisture and weather forecasts
  • Remote monitoring and control via smartphone apps
  • Water usage analytics and optimization
  • Integration with weather stations for real-time adjustments

Get Started

  1. Install soil moisture sensors in your field
  2. Connect to a smart irrigation controller (e.g., Rachio)
  3. Monitor and adjust via your phone or computer
